Reimbursement Policy & Procedure

Overview

This portal facilitates the process for requesting reimbursements or pre-approval for purchases made on behalf of Silicon Valley Academy (SVA). Please review the policy tiers carefully before submitting your request. All requests require appropriate documentation (receipts, quotes, or prior approval emails). For questions, contact admin@svamail.com.

Expense Tiers & Approval Requirements

Tier 1: Up to $200

  • **No Prior Approval Required:** For routine, small expenses (e.g., classroom supplies).
  • **Payment Method:** Can use personal funds (submit for reimbursement) or request school account use if applicable.
  • **Documentation:** Requires a clear receipt or invoice.
  • **Submission:** Submit via the "New Request" tab. Reimbursements are typically auto-approved upon submission if documentation is valid.

Tier 2: $200.01 - $1000

  • **Admin Pre-Approval Required:** Must obtain approval from a school administrator **before** making the purchase.
  • **Payment Method:** Can use personal funds (submit for reimbursement after pre-approval) or request school account use.
  • **Documentation:** Requires the **pre-approval email/documentation** AND the final receipt/invoice (for reimbursement) or quote (for school payment).
  • **Submission:** Submit via the "New Request" tab. Select "Pre-Approval Request" initially. Once pre-approved, submit again selecting "Reimbursement" or "School Payment Request".
  • **Approval Process:** After submission, requires formal approval by an Admin within the portal (even if pre-approved via email).

Tier 3: $1000.01 - $5000

  • **Admin AND Board Pre-Approval Required:** Must obtain approval from both a school administrator AND a designated board member **before** making the purchase.
  • **Payment Method:** **MUST use school account.** Personal funds cannot be used for reimbursement in this tier.
  • **Documentation:** Requires the **Admin & Board pre-approval documentation** AND the vendor quote/invoice.
  • **Submission:** Submit via the "New Request" tab. Select "Pre-Approval Request" initially. Once pre-approved, submit again selecting "School Payment Request".
  • **Approval Process:** After submission, requires formal approval by both an Admin and a Board Member within the portal.

Over $5000

Expenses exceeding $5000 cannot be processed through this portal. Please contact the Board of Directors directly at bod@svamail.com with detailed justification and quotes for consideration.

General Guidelines

  • **Timeliness:** Reimbursement requests should be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date.
  • **Documentation:** All submissions require clear, itemized receipts or invoices. Quotes are required for pre-approval requests involving school funds. Attachments must be clear and legible.
  • **Approval Timeframe:** Approvers (Admin/Board) are expected to review requests within 5 business days.
  • **Payment:** Approved reimbursements are typically processed within 1-2 weeks after final approval. School account payments are made directly to the vendor based on the approved quote/invoice.
  • **Non-Compliance:** Requests submitted without required pre-approvals or documentation may be delayed or denied.
